I am **Zhang Jingxuan**, an M.Sc. student in **AI for Science** at the **National University of Singapore** (Faculty of Science, Department of Physics). I received my B.Eng. in **Software Engineering** from **Beihang University**.

My interests include graph neural networks, complex systems, and data-driven methods for scientific discovery.

## Education
